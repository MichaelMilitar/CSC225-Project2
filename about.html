About

Twoms was founded in 2019 by Michael Militar. The name was created using his own name. Twoms' ideology is high quality clothes should be accessible for everyone regardless of price and size so our clothing will always be priced reasonably and come in several different sizes. Michael's future vision is to create an online clothing store where people are virtually able to try on clothes.
